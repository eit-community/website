EIT Community website

This web site is aimed at encouraging communication among members of the Electrical Impedance Tomography (EIT) community.



We are community of

  • 500+ researchers
  • 13+ Research labs

in 8 countries on 3 continents

The site includes
  • a mailing list which we hope will be actively used for discussion,
  • announcements of EIT Community meetings i.e., connferences,
  • a list of EIT research groups,
  • a list of Research & Commercial EIT hardware & Software Tools
  • a list of publications i.e, special issues & conference proceedings
  • a list of vacancies of interest to the EIT community
  • …
